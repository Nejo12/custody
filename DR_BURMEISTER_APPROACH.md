### 1. **Separate the Personal from the Pitch**

- **Step 1 = Human Connection** (custody battle affecting you)

- Don't mix them in the same conversation

- You're asking for **help**, not making a **demand**
- If he can't help, that's OK - he's still your criminal lawyer

### 3. **Gratitude Always**

- Thank him for his time, regardless of outcome
- He's already helping you with a serious case

### 4. **Don't Jeopardize the Criminal Case Relationship**

- Your racial discrimination case is critical
- Don't let the custody tool conversation distract from that
- If he seems annoyed or uninterested, drop it immediately
